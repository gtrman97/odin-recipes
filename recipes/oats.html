overnight oats

This breakfast classic has been a long time staple for me.

Ingredients

  • flaxseed meal
  • brown sugar
  • cinnamon
  • steel cut oats
  • almond milk
  • frozen wild bluberries
  • frozen organic kale

directions

  1. pour 1/2 tbsp flaxseed meal into meal prep container
  2. pour in one spoonful brown sugar
  3. sprinkle in cinnamon to your liking
  4. pour in 1/2 cup steels cut oats
  5. pour in 1 cup almond milk
  6. put 1/2 cup wild bluberries in
  7. put 1/2 cup frozen kale in
  8. make sure bluberries and kale are separated and submerged in milk
  9. cover and let sit overnight
  10. enjoy in the morning!
