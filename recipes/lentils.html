Lentil Soup

This hearty lentil soup with potatoes kept me going throughout my senior year at Texas State.

Ingredients

  • lentils
  • potatoes
  • tomatoes
  • vegetable broth
  • salt

directions

  1. pour lentils in crockpot
  2. dice up potatoes
  3. throw potatoes in crockpot
  4. pour diced tomatoes on top
  5. pour half gallon of water in and let lentils soak for about an hour
  6. pour vegetable broth over everything and turn crockpot on low
  7. let cook for 7-8 hours
  8. serve and sprinkle salt on top for taste
