loaded baked potatoes

This is a skillet style version of loaded baked potatoes.

Ingredients

  • russet potatoes
  • black beans
  • mild salsa or tomatoes
  • salt

directions

  1. dice the potatoes and put them in the crockpot
  2. pour in two cans of black beans
  3. cook on low for 5 hours
  4. stir in tomatoes towards the end
  5. sprinkle on some salt
  6. enjoy!
